Hot Pockets!

Description

Here we have the Odin Project's most famous recipe in the world, the hot pocket snack! First introduced in the early days of 2009, the hot pocket was a revolutionary snackers item and has since been known as one of the most popular choices among new customers. Loved for its form factor as a wrap-like treat with warm, tasty innards such as pepporoni, meatballs, ham and cheese, it's a meal worth drooling over! Try today!

Ingredients Needed:

  • Hot Pockets from the store
  • A little love from your heart
  • Seasoning???

Steps

  1. Open Hot Pockets from box
  2. Take out Hot Pocket from box
  3. Put Hot Pocket in box... I mean microwave
  4. Leave Hot Pocket in microwave for at least one minute (or two if you are edgy)
  5. Take out Hot Pocket after done in microwave and unwrap onto plate
  6. Serve and enjoy!
