Pizza Rolls!

Description

Here we have the Odin Project's best comfort recipe by popular vote, pizza rolls snackers! First introduced in 2011, the pizza rolls recipe was an overnight sensation among family crowds. A tasty pizza-like morsel with a beautiful bready crust, you're sure to enjoy just about every bite. Cook it up today!

Ingredients Needed:

  • Pizza Rolls from frozen section of store
  • A little pep in your step
  • A dipping sauce???

Steps

  1. Open Pizza Rolls box and take out Pizza Rolls in bag
  2. Open Pizza Rolls bag
  3. Pour out desired serving size of Pizza Rolls
  4. Put Pizza Rolls in microwave
  5. Leave Pizza Rolls in microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds
  6. Take out Pizza Rolls after done in microwave and enjoy immediately
